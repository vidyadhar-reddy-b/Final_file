📖 Our Story
How Click2Book Started

Click2Book was founded in 2016 by a team of passionate travelers who were frustrated with the complicated, unreliable bus booking experience in India. We believed that technology could solve this — making bus travel as easy as ordering food online.

Starting with just 3 routes, we've grown to cover 250+ routes across 15+ cities, partnering with India's best bus operators to deliver a premium, hassle-free booking experience to millions of customers.

Today, Click2Book is India's most trusted bus booking platform with a 4.8-star rating and over 10 lakh satisfied travelers who choose us for every journey.
