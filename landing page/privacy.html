📋Your Privacy Platform to Us

Click2Book Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our bus booking platform.

⚠️ By using Click2Book, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. Please read it carefully.

📥Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our services:

  • Personal identification: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Booking details: Origin, destination, travel date, seat preferences, passenger information
  • Payment information: We store only the last 4 digits and card type — full payment data is processed by certified gateways
  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, OS, pages visited, time spent, referral source
  • Location data: City-level location (with consent) to suggest nearby pick-up points

⚙️How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve our services:

  • To process your bus ticket bookings and send e-ticket confirmations
  • To send booking reminders, departure alerts, and trip updates via SMS/email
  • To process refunds and handle cancellation requests efficiently
  • To personalize your experience with route recommendations and relevant offers
  • To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and security incidents
  • To improve our platform based on aggregated, anonymized usage analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

🔗Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Bus Operators: We share passenger name, phone, and seat details with the relevant operator to fulfill your booking
  • Payment Processors: We share transaction data with PCI-DSS certified payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU) to process payments securely
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity under the same privacy protections

🛡️Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3 / 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Payment card data is never stored on our servers — processed by PCI-DSS Level 1 gateways only
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt — never stored in plain text
  • Regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments are conducted
  • Access to user data within Click2Book is role-based and strictly limited to authorized personnel

✅Your Rights & Choices

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information via your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format (CSV/JSON)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the email footer link
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances
To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@click2book.in with the subject "Data Request – [Your Right]".

📁Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Booking records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes
  • Payment transaction logs: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian financial regulations
  • Support correspondence: Retained for 2 years after resolution
  • Analytics data: Retained in anonymized, aggregated form indefinitely for platform improvement

🍪Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on Click2Book:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions, booking flow, and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your city preferences and search history for faster booking
  • Marketing Cookies: Used (with consent) to show relevant offers. You can opt out at any time

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

🔄Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Sending a notification email to your registered email address
  • Displaying a prominent banner on the Click2Book platform for 30 days
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Your continued use of Click2Book after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
